The future of our practice and craft begins now,

It is the responsibility of thou.

I’m not talking space ships, LED’s and special effects,

We’re looking at those issues concerning the global population, whom we really affect.

There have been many brilliant pieces of art and prose,

Held up as shining examples, completed by unknown those.

Perhaps this is the future I see,

Where practicing is not about a single project; or a star-chitect or three.

It is a big shock! I know! I know!

But think of it as a chance for us all to grow.

Some worry that in future with those in building we cannot compete;

Some fear our craft being shipped offshore & becoming obsolete.

But; to those I say—never fear my friend,

You don’t get into this business simply to construct & mend!

Thanks to a fruit, we can create and collaborate together or apart,

These new type of projects we will be delving into are not those where the client is the start.

People may work on guerrilla projects,

Using their skills to solve the issues that policy neglects.

Architecture becomes about the seed or idea,

A new way of thinking & considering problems, and not just those in Eritrea!

Because really we didn’t start for the biz at all…

But because our minds are curious about things big and small.

Together with others outside our field,

We redesign our cities, economy & future; the answers no longer concealed.

How you say? Well that is up to you!

I simply hope this little poem is causative; the discussion not perdue!

- Architect anon.
